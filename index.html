Common questions

Do I need to know anything technical?

Not a thing. You tell us about your business; we handle everything — design, words, photos, hosting.

What do you need from me?

Your business name, what you offer, a phone number, and any photos or logo you have. That's it.

Can I use my own website address (domain)?

Yes — we can set up a custom address like yourbusiness.com, or you can start with a free link right away.

What if I want changes later?

Every site includes 2 rounds of changes. After that, the optional Care plan covers unlimited small edits.
