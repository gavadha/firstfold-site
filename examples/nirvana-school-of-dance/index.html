Meet Swetha Sridhara

Founder and lead choreographer of Nirvana School of Dance, Swetha brings years of stage and teaching experience to every class. She built Nirvana to give women across New Jersey a space to dance freely, build confidence, and celebrate Bollywood culture together — whether you're 5 or 65, brand new or a natural.

Every class is welcoming, high-energy, and tailored so everyone keeps up and has a blast.
